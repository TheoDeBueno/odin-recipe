Oatmeal with egg and veggies

Easy to make oatmeal rich in protein and fibre

Ingredients

  • Rolled oats
  • 1 egg
  • Spinach or bok choy
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • Olive oil
  • Salt
  • Black pepper

Steps

  1. Cook the rolled oats with water according to the package instructions.
  2. Heat a small amount of olive oil in a pan and saut√© the minced garlic until fragrant.
  3. Add the vegetables and cook until softened.
  4. Crack the egg into the pan and scramble it gently with the vegetables.
  5. Add the cooked oatmeal to the pan and mix well.
  6. Season with salt and black pepper, then serve warm.
