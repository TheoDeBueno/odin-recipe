Whole Wheat Tuna Avocado Toast

Ingredients

  • 2 slices whole wheat bread
  • 1 can tuna (in water), drained
  • 1/2 avocado, mashed
  • 1/4 onion or cucumber, thinly sliced
  • 1 tsp lemon juice
  • Black pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Toast the whole wheat bread slices until lightly browned.
  2. In a small bowl, mash the avocado and mix with lemon juice and black pepper.
  3. Spread the mashed avocado evenly on each toast slice.
  4. Top with drained tuna.
  5. Add thinly sliced onion or cucumber on top.
  6. Season with a little extra black pepper if desired, then serve immediately.
