teochewfish

This is the teochewfish, a dish originated from China that flavours in a hint of sour kick with umami flavours, good nutrition in one dish, yum.

Ingredients
  • Fish (pomfret,sea bass, snapper)
  • Ginger
  • Tomato diced
  • Sour plum
  • Salted mustard greens
  • Garlic
  • Chili (optional)

Steps
  1. Clean the fish, remove the scales and organs, make shallow cuts on both sides, lightly salt it, then rinse and pat dry.
  2. Prepare the ingredients by cutting the tomatoes, slicing the ginger, lightly crushing the garlic, and cracking the preserved sour plums.
  3. Heat oil in a pot and saut√© the ginger and garlic until fragrant.
  4. Add preserved vegetables, tomatoes, and sour plums, then stir briefly.
  5. Pour in water or stock, bring to a boil, and season with salt and white pepper.
  6. Gently add the fish, cover, and simmer for 8 to 10 minutes until fully cooked, then garnish and serve.
